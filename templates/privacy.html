Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What We Collect

  • Your Discord user ID and display name.
  • Your linked Ubisoft username (public, used to fetch your Rainbow Six Siege stats).
  • Match statistics (kills, deaths, wins, losses, headshots, and related in-game stats) tied to your Discord user ID and linked Ubisoft username.
  • The Discord server (guild) ID(s) you're tracked in, so your stats stay scoped to the correct server(s).
  • Billing and subscription details when you purchase a seat or subscription — handled entirely by our payment processor (Lemon Squeezy); we do not directly collect or store your card details.

2. How We Use It

Solely to provide the tracking/leaderboard service: fetching your public Rainbow Six Siege stats, computing session/monthly/season/lifetime totals, checking for records, and posting them to the Discord server(s) you're tracked in. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside operating BreachReportBot.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. It is shared only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Discord — your stats, leaderboards, and records are posted into the Discord server(s) you're tracked in, visible to members of that server.
  • r6data — a third-party Rainbow Six Siege stats API we query using your linked Ubisoft username to fetch your public in-game stats.
  • Lemon Squeezy — our payment processor, which handles checkout, billing, and subscription management for seat purchases. We receive subscription status from them but never your full payment details.

4. Data Retention

We retain your linked-account data for as long as you're actively tracked. If a seat is unassigned or a subscription is cancelled, tracking for the affected account stops immediately, and the live link is removed after a retention period (30 days for a reassigned/paused account, 7 days for a cancelled one). Historical stats already recorded — monthly, season, and lifetime totals, and any records set — are preserved after this, similar to how a retired athlete still holds their records; only the active tracking relationship is removed.

5. Requesting Deletion of Your Data

You can request deletion of your linked Discord/Ubisoft account data at any time by contacting us at support@breachreportbot.com. We will remove your linked account, stop tracking you, and delete the association between your Discord user ID and your Ubisoft username. As noted above, already-recorded historical stats and records may be retained in anonymized/aggregate form unless you specifically request their removal too — let us know in your request if you'd like those removed as well.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage and restricted access to our database and hosting infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access.

7. Not Affiliated with Ubisoft

BreachReportBot is an independent, unofficial tool. It is not produced by, endorsed by, or affiliated with Ubisoft Entertainment.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy, or requests regarding your data, can be sent to support@breachreportbot.com.
