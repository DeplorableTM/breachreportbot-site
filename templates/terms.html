1. The Service

BreachReportBot ("the Service") is a Discord bot that tracks Rainbow Six Siege statistics for players who link their Ubisoft account, and posts leaderboards, records, and session summaries into a Discord server. 3. Accounts and Seats

Access to tracking for a given player is granted via a purchased "seat" (or an "unlimited" grant covering an entire server or user). A seat covers exactly one linked Ubisoft account, for one Discord user, in one specific server. The purchaser of a seat retains control of it — including which Discord user it covers and which server it's assigned to — regardless of who it's currently assigned to, and may reassign or unassign it at any time.

4. Subscriptions and Billing

Subscriptions are billed on a recurring basis through our payment processor, Lemon Squeezy. Where a free trial is offered, it will automatically convert to a paid subscription at the end of the trial period unless cancelled beforehand. You can cancel a subscription at any time; cancellation stops future billing but does not retroactively refund amounts already charged, except as described in our Refund Policy.

5. Data Retention

When a seat is unassigned or a subscription is cancelled, the affected linked account stops being tracked immediately. Historical stats already recorded (monthly, season, and lifetime totals, and any records set) are retained; the live tracking link itself is removed after a retention period (30 days for a reassigned/paused account, 7 days for a cancelled one). See our Privacy Policy for full detail on data handling and how to request deletion.
