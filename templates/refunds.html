Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

BreachReportBot is operated by Nine18, LLC ("we," "us," "our").

Where you purchased your subscription determines which refund process applies — see below.

Subscriptions Purchased Through Our Website (Lemon Squeezy)

If you're not satisfied with BreachReportBot, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase. Refunds are not provided for renewal charges after the first billing cycle — cancel before your next renewal date to avoid being charged again. Cancelling stops future billing but does not retroactively refund the current billing period unless it falls within the 14-day window above.

Subscriptions Purchased Through Discord's Store

If you subscribed directly through Discord (via our bot's Store page or a Premium purchase button in Discord), your purchase and any refund request are handled entirely by Discord, not by us — we're not able to issue or override refund decisions for these purchases. Discord determines refund eligibility under its own Monetization Terms and Refund Policy. To request a refund for a Discord-purchased subscription, please contact Discord support directly rather than us.

Trials

Where a free trial is offered, no payment is taken during the trial period. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, it automatically converts to a paid subscription and billing begins. You can cancel at any time during the trial with no charge.

Exceptions

For purchases made through our website, refunds may be denied in cases of suspected abuse (e.g., repeatedly subscribing and requesting refunds) or where required by our payment processor's own policies.

How to Request a Refund

For website (Lemon Squeezy) purchases: email deplorable.tm@gmail.com with your purchase details (the Discord account or server the subscription is tied to). We aim to process eligible refund requests within 5 business days.

For Discord Store purchases: please contact Discord support directly — we cannot process these refunds ourselves.
